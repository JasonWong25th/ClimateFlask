About this globe

The globe of extremes

Where is the most distant point from land? What altitude does the highest permanent human settlement have? Who was the first to reach the lowest point on Earth? Click on the orange icons on this globe to find out!

3D Globe created by John and Raluca after getting inspired by the Half-Earth Project globe, and this awesome globe and this otherawesome globe. We used ArcGIS API for JavaScript to create the interactive globe and ArcGIS Pro to create the basemap. Many thanks to Arno Fiva, Christian Iten and Jesse van den Kieboom for the awesome feedback.

Read this blog post to find out how the globe was created. For the curious coders, here is the Github repository.
